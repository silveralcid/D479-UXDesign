Transportation

Getting Here

Most visitors arrive in Taniti by air, landing at our charming airport designed for small jets and propeller planes. For the adventurous, a small cruise ship docks at Yellow Leaf Bay once a week, offering a unique arrival experience. Exciting developments are underway, with plans to expand our airport to welcome larger jets in the near future!

Ground Transportation

Getting Around

Taniti City is well-connected by public buses running daily from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m., making it easy to get around. For those venturing beyond the city, private buses, taxis, and rental cars are readily available. If you prefer a more active approach, rent a bike from one of our local vendors and explore the flat, walkable streets of Taniti City or the scenic paths around Merriton Landing. Helmets are provided and required by law, ensuring a safe and enjoyable ride!
